What is Expanse?

Contracts & DApps

What if you could write applications that didnt have a single point of failure and their computational overhead was shared among the users? What if you could write a legal contract that was shared among a plethora of people. No more back and forth with lawyers. Expanse contracts work exactly as written. No fungibility in interpreting clauses. No failing to uphld ends without repercussions.

Decentralized EVERYTHING.

The internet was never meant to be the centralized failure prone monolith contrived mess it is today. The idea was to have everything decentralized. No single entity would hold all the power or all the data free to sell to the highest bidder. Random agencies would be able to shut down valued services used by millions. We can make that dream a possibility, finally.

Fast. Intuitive. Poweful.

Expanse wants to make it easy to develop powerful decentralized applications and systems. Unlike other competitors with their own domain specific languages that are strange and aggravating to work with for the laymen programmer. We know we can do better.

Community

Expanse is a community driven project. It arose out of the cryptocurrency communites aggravation at the centralized tactics of our competitiors who claimed to be decentralized. Spearheaded by well known cyptocurrency community members with an insight into what people want and open to feedback, code and priorities set by the large and exponentially growing community.
